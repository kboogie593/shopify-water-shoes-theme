# FurQuiet Product Development and Supplier QA

Updated: 2026-06-01

## Product Manager Brief

The first product is a quiet-start grooming vacuum kit for heavy-shedding dog and long-hair cat homes. The buyer problem is not "pet grooming" in general. It is loose fur spreading across sofas, rugs, clothes, bedding, and small apartments after ordinary brushing.

The product must prove four things:

1. It catches loose fur while brushing.
2. It can be introduced gradually to nervous pets.
3. It is easy to empty, clean, and store.
4. It is safe and correctly labeled for the launch market.

## 1688 Supplier Screening

Score each supplier from 1 to 5:

| Dimension | What To Check | Reject If |
|---|---|---|
| Product match | Pet grooming vacuum, hose, brush heads, clipper support, dust cup | Only handheld lint remover or no pet-specific kit |
| Certification | US/CA/UK/AU plug options, voltage, CE/FCC/UKCA/UL/ETL evidence where relevant | No documentation or inconsistent labels |
| Sample access | Can ship 1-3 samples with all accessories | MOQ-only, no sample, unclear photos |
| Replacement parts | Filters, brush heads, clipper guards, hose | No filter supply or no accessory SKUs |
| Noise proof | Supplier can provide decibel test or we can measure sample | Claims exact low noise with no test |
| Packaging | English manual, carton dimensions, insert protection | No manual or fragile packing |
| Media rights | Permission to use supplier photos temporarily or sample filming plan | Watermarked competitor media only |
| Lead time | First batch, replenishment, holiday cutoff | No clear production timeline |

## Sample Test Script

Run these tests before opening serious checkout volume.

### Unboxing

- Photograph box, inserts, manual, labels, plugs, spare filters, and all attachments.
- Confirm the kit contents match the PDP and CSV import.
- Weigh the unit and final packed box.
- Measure carton dimensions for shipping cost.

### Noise

- Measure background room noise.
- Measure unit at low and high settings from 1 ft and 3 ft.
- Record one video with the pet not present and one gradual introduction video.
- Do not publish exact decibel claims until readings are repeated and documented.

### Grooming Flow

- Tool off near pet.
- Unit on several feet away.
- Brush pass with suction off.
- Brush pass on low airflow.
- Short fur cup reveal.
- Empty dust cup and clean brush head.

### Durability

- Run 10-minute session.
- Check hose connection, clipper heat, dust cup fit, button feel, cord strain, filter clogging.
- Repeat empty/attach cycle 20 times.

### Compliance and Trust

- Confirm voltage and plug variant by launch market.
- Confirm manual warnings and safe-use wording.
- Confirm return policy can handle used pet grooming products.
- Confirm whether filters are washable or replacement-only.

## Claims Policy

Safe claims before sample test:

- Helps collect loose fur during routine brushing.
- Designed for quiet-start introduction.
- Built for heavy-shedding homes.
- Useful for reducing loose fur before it reaches furniture.

Do not claim yet:

- Exact decibel level.
- Medical/anxiety treatment.
- Stops shedding.
- Replaces professional grooming.
- Works for every pet.
- Verified rating or customer review count.

## Launch Decision

Open checkout only when:

- Product media is uploaded.
- Shipping and return costs are modeled.
- The PDP has real sample photos or clearly marked render/brand images.
- Supplier sample passes the tests above.
- The first 30 social videos have been validated for comments and hooks.
