# FurQuiet Product Copy

## Product Title

FurQuiet Grooming Vacuum Kit

## Short Description

FurQuiet helps heavy-shedding dog and long-hair cat homes brush loose fur and collect it in the dust cup before it reaches the sofa, rug, bedding, or clothes.

## Product Description

FurQuiet is a quiet-start pet grooming vacuum kit for routine at-home brushing, de-shedding, trimming support, and post-grooming cleanup.

The kit pairs a compact vacuum base with a transparent dust cup, flexible hose, de-shedding brush, slicker brush, clipper body, clipper guards, narrow nozzle, washable filter care, cleaning brush, and storage dock. The buyer promise is simple: introduce the sound gradually, brush slowly, collect loose fur visibly, and reset the room faster.

Best for huskies, German shepherds, golden retrievers, corgis, Samoyeds, mixed double-coat dogs, multi-pet homes, and long-hair cats.

## Key Bullets

- Low-noise grooming workflow for nervous pets
- De-shedding brush captures loose undercoat into the dust cup
- Clipper support for paws, belly, and sanitary-area maintenance
- Transparent dust cup creates visible proof for product photos and short videos
- Washable filter care and compact storage dock for small homes
- Built for routine brushing between professional grooming visits
